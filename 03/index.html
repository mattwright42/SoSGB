Prepping for the BazaarBazaar DaysRanking Up the Bazaar

  • Ready Your Wares

    Bazaars are held every Saturday. Spend your week collecting and crafting the goods you want to sell, then head over to the bazaar grounds.

  • Setting Your Stock

    Check in at your stall to set up your stock. Simply select the items you want to sell from your bag and place them in your stock. You can adjust your stock any time before opening up shop.

  • Trending Items

    Customers keep an eye out for seasonal trends, so keep those items in mind when selecting your wares. Trending items sell faster and for higher prices!

  • Bazaar Décor

    Before opening shop, decorate your stall and make use of décor bonuses. Décor will give you a variety of buff effects, including those that make items sell faster or build up more Happy Energy.

  • Tent Series Effects

    Your tent is the face of your stall! The more décor you display from the same series as your tent, the more they'll impact your sales. You can have up to three tent series effects active at once.

  • Opening Up Shop

    When it's time to open shop, head to your stall in the northeast area of the bazaar grounds. Each bazaar day is split into two shifts, one in the morning and one in the afternoon.

  • Your Counter

    Once a bazaar shift starts, press the X Button at your counter to select items from your stock to put on display.

  • Helping Customers

    Potential customers will approach your counter and ask for a quantity of a specific item. Move over to the product they want and press the A Button to sell each item.

  • Attracting Crowds

    Some customers might not notice you or your stall. Press the ZR Button to ring your bell and get their attention. Doing so with a lot of customers nearby is sure to turn heads!

  • Nature Sprite Cheer Squad

    Once you meet the Nature Sprites, you'll eventually be able to call for them as your personal bazaar cheer squad. Their cheers grant all sorts of useful buffs as you sell.

  • The Cheer Gauge

    Sell products to build up your Cheer Gauge. Fill up the gauge at least once, then press the ZL Button to start Cheer Time. Fill the gauge five times to call in the power of the full Cheer Squad!

  • Cheer Time

    Cheer Time is your chance to sell as much as you can by simply pressing the A Button. Your counter will also replenish itself using items from your stock even if they weren't displayed.

  • Cheer Effects

    Each Nature Sprite will bestow a unique effect during Cheer Time. The higher their level, the greater their effect.

  • Shopping Spree

    Make sure to stop by other shops at the bazaar! Stalls like Felix's Fixings and the Happy Shoppe have rare and exclusive goods you can't buy anywhere else.

  • Felix's Techniques

    Visit Felix's Fixings to learn useful new techniques. From double jumping to advanced fishing methods, the mayor is eager to share his wealth of knowledge!

  • The Happy Shoppe

    This shop is run by the Nature Sprites and only accepts Happy Energy. They offer all sorts of mystical items, including storage portals you can access from your bazaar stall and other places across town.

  • Bazaar Sales

    A summary of your sales will be shown after each bazaar shift. The Bazaar Review Board sends their review the following morning, issuing an increase in rank if your bazaar met its goals.

  • Bazaar Rank

    The true mark of a bazaar's notoriety is its bazaar rank. More customers will flock to bazaars with higher rank, as well as prospective new vendors.

  • Rank-Up Goals

    The bazaar review includes benchmarks needed to reach the next rank. Both the bazaar's total sales and total number of stalls are taken into account.

  • Opening New Stalls

    As your bazaar rank increases, more prospective vendors will want to open a stall of their own. Help resolve whatever hurdles they face so they can set up shop!
