  • Getting Acquainted

    Zephyr Town is a vibrant community full of interesting folks to meet. Talking to people once a day and giving them gifts is a sure way to strengthen your friendships with them.

  • Friendship Levels

    Check your friendship levels in the Resident Info tab of your bag menu. The more hearts you have filled with someone, the stronger your bond—and the more special moments you'll share.

  • Locked Hearts

    If someone's friendship level is shown with a lock icon, the relationship will not progress further until certain conditions are met.

  • Love & Friendship

    After a relationship progresses to a set level, you'll be able to express your true feelings for them. Forge a lasting friendship or follow your heart and confess your love.

  • Proposing

    You can propose marriage to your newfound love or trusted friend by presenting them a blue feather. If they accept, you'll begin living together and have the chance to raise a family.

  • New Faces

    As time goes on, new faces may move into town. Make sure to give them a warm welcome when they do!

  • Growing Farm, Growing Town

    As you get to know the community, you'll find more ways to help your farm and the town grow—whether it's breaking new ground for field expansions or repairing the broken-down windmills.

  • Town Facilities

    Zephyr Town's streets are lined with homes, shops, windmills, and other facilities for you to explore. Jump on the barrels scattered across town for a quick shortcut!

  • Shops

    Stop by Miguel's Mercantile for your general buying and selling needs, then head to Café Madeleine for a bite to eat. Each shop has its own set business hours.

  • Explore Every Inch

    Curiosity has its perks! Take a peek at bookshelves, landmarks, and other places that catch your eye, and you may find hidden recipes and medals.

  • The Glider

    As your story progresses, you'll receive a glider that lets you soar through the skies. Utilize the wind to explore new areas and drift through town with ease.

  • Using Your Glider

    Hold down the B Button while jumping to open your glider. Adjust your flight path with the Left Stick, then release the B Button to drop back down to the ground.

  • Mind the Wind

    Wind strength will greatly impact your gliding. A strong tailwind will help you glide farther, but gliding against the wind will make it hard to go anywhere.

  • Catching Bugs

    Little bugs and critters call the areas around Zephyr Town home. Approach slowly and press the A Button to catch them. A good jump can also help you catch bugs mid-flight.

  • Fishing

    Equip your fishing rod near a body of water to start fishing. Once the bobber sinks, press the Y Button to start reeling in your catch. Attaching bait will make certain kinds of fish more likely to bite.

  • Landing the Catch

    A fish's stamina is shown with fish-shaped icons. Follow the prompts shown on the screen with the Left Stick to deplete its stamina before your fishing rod's durability gauge runs out.

  • Festivals

    Zephyr Town hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year. Whether you're celebrating the start of a new season or showing off your cooking skills, there's a festival for the occasion.

  • Crop & Animal Shows

    Have your crops and animals judged in these seasonal competitions. Higher quality crops and more affectionate animals are key to gaining a better score.

  • Horse Derby

    Once you have a horse of your own, head to the Equestrian Park and train for this biannual horseback riding competition.

  • Pet Show

    Pet Shows are a test of your bond with your faithful companion. Follow the button prompts to have your pet perform the right tricks.

  • Prizes

    Winners of local competitions and shows receive trophies and prizes. These trophies can be displayed on the shelves in your house.

  • The Mountains

    In time, you'll be able to explore the mountainous area outside of town. Here you'll find rare ore, bugs, and fish, as well as space to take up beekeeping and mushroom cultivating.

  • Mushroom Cultivating

    Plant spores on mushroom logs, then harvest your freshly grown mushrooms a few days later.

  • Beekeeping

    Plant fully grown, in-season flowers in your flower bed, then collect honeycombs from the beehive a few days later. The honeycombs you collect vary depending on the types of flowers planted.
